About Me

Hi, I’m Hunter Cartier, and I’m passionate about IT. I love the world of computers, networking, and Linux. My daily driver is Linux Mint, which gives me the freedom I need to explore and customize. I also enjoy bash scripting—it’s like solving puzzles to automate tasks and make repetitive tasks easier. I’m always curious about how things work, whether it’s using new tools or deepening my understanding of networking protocols. Open-source software is a huge inspiration for me; I appreciate the collaborative spirit and the endless opportunities for learning it provides. I think free and open-source software is important for privacy and the free(dom) to do what you want with it. I just want to continue to learn more about information technology as that takes up the majority of my free time.
